Responsible Gaming

Understanding Gambling Risks

Steak is a simulator designed to mimic the experience of online gambling without using real money. However, it's important to understand the risks associated with gambling:

  • Gambling can be addictive and lead to financial problems
  • The odds are always in favor of the house in the long run
  • Chasing losses is a common behavior that leads to bigger losses
  • Gambling should never be seen as a way to make money

Healthy Gaming Habits

To maintain a healthy relationship with gaming:

  • Set time limits for your gaming sessions
  • Never gamble with money you can't afford to lose
  • Take regular breaks during gaming sessions
  • Balance gaming with other activities and responsibilities

Getting Help

If you or someone you know is struggling with gambling addiction, help is available:

  • National Problem Gambling Helpline: 1-800-522-4700
  • Gamblers Anonymous: www.gamblersanonymous.org
  • National Council on Problem Gambling: www.ncpgambling.org

Self-Assessment

Answer these questions honestly to assess your gaming habits:

1. Do you often gamble longer than you intended?

2. Have you ever lied to cover up the amount of time or money you spend gambling?

3. Have you ever felt guilty after gambling?

4. Have you ever borrowed money or sold anything to get money to gamble?

5. Has gambling affected your relationships or work?
